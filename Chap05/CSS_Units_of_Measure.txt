% - Relative percentages - 100% relative to its container
pt - points, there are are 72 points in an inch
pc - pica - 12 points in 1 pica
em - setting of the current font -- 1 em is 12pt, if the font is set to 12pt, 2 em is 24
ex - half the size of an em 
in - imperial inch size. relative size, depends on the graphics enviornment
cm - centermeters
mm - millimeter
px - pixels - are usually 1:1, but in retina screen pixels are 2:1
